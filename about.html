About the Intramurals

The MPSU Intramurals 2025 is a grand celebration that brings together students, faculty, and staff to showcase unity, sportsmanship, and school pride. Through friendly competition and teamwork, the event fosters lasting camaraderie and highlights the athletic excellence of MPSUians.
